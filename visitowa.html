Pre-K Season Pass

Children age 3 and under receive a FREE Pre- K season pass valid for Tropic Falls. Parent or guardian must still secure a Day Pass or Season Pass for access to theme park and waterpark attractions.

* Valid for use in Tropic Falls upon activation; expires 12/31/2023. Indoor Waterpark and Outdoor Waterpark (currently under construction) access also included.

In-person only

Senior and Military Discount

Senior discounts of 20% off are available on tickets for those 55 and older with a valid ID. Limit one discounted Combo Ticket or Season Pass per ID. 15% Military discounts are available on tickets with a valid ID. Limit 6 tickets total with valid ID. Limit of 1 Season Pass per valid ID.

* Discounts cannot be combined with other special offers, coupons or discounts.
